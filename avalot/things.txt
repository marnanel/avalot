THINGS.TXT
""""""""""

Things you can pick up in Avalot.

  1 : Wine

Get: 1) from pub in Argent. This wine deteriorates over time, becoming
        nasty wine, then vinegar.

     2) from pub in Nottingham. This wine stays good always.

Use: 1) for pickling the onion,
     2) for drinking (get the key.)
		(NB: You may also drink beer, ale, whisky, cider, etc.)

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

  2 : Money

Get: Ab initio.

Use: Paying for things.

(Note: if you lose this you lose the game.)

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

  3 : Bodkin

Get: Ab initio.

Use: Red herring.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

  4 : Potion

Get: From Spludwick, after you've given him the onion, the ink and the
mushroom, in that order.

Use: Transforms Arkata (at the end of the game.)

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -


  5 : Chastity belt.

Get: From Geida.

Use: ?

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

  6 : Bolt

Get: From Cardiff (jump to get it; it's buried in the wall.)

Use: Used with the crossbow to give to Cwytalot.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

  7 : Crossbow

Get: From Robin Hood, after winning shoot-'em-up.

Use: Same as Bolt.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

  8 : Lute

Get: From Dogfood, in the pub, after winning Nim.

Use: Give to Geida to play lullaby to Baron du Lustie.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

  9 : Pilgrim's badge.

Get: From Roamalot, via Crapulus, near the start of the game.

Use: Give to Ibythneth in the pub.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

 10 : Mushroom

Use: Give to Spludwick.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

 11 : Key (to chastity belt.)

Get: After drinking more than seven pints, you wake up holding this.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

 12 : Bell

Get: Ab initio.

Use: Red herring (again.)

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

 13 : Prescription

Get: From Dr. Duck in Norwich.

Use: Give to Spludwick.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

 14 : Pen

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

 15 : Ink

Use: Give to Spludwick.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

 16 : Clothes

Get: Ab initio.

Use: Normal wear.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

 17 : Habit

Get: From Ibythneth in the pub.

Use: Get into Abbey.

        -   -   -   -   -   -   -

 18 : Onion

Get: From trader in market in Norwich.

Use: Give to Spludwick.
